Practice fingerspelling in American Sign Language.

Instructions: A word will be fingerspelled for you on the left-stage. Type the word you see. For each letter that you type, you will see its sign on the right-stage.

If you need to see the word again, leave the textbox blank and press the button. When you are ready to submit your answer, press the button. You may alternatively hit the [ENTER] key instead of pressing the button.

If you get a word right, your score increases by 1. If you get a word wrong, your score decreases by 1. There is no penalty or reward for replaying a word.

You can increase the fingerspelling speed by pressing the button, or by sliding the speed slider to the right.

You can decrease the fingerspelling speed by pressing the button, or by sliding the speed slider to the left.

By default, when you get a word right, the speed will increase automatically and when you get a word wrong, the speed will decrease automatically. This setting can be disabled by unchecking the Auto Save checkbox.

By default, you will see words that are 5 letters long or less. This can be changed by typing in a new word length limit and pressing the button. Note that the smallest word length is 3 letters.
